About Me

Hello! I'm Akram Albayhaqi, a 25-year-old multimedia enthusiast with a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia Education from Indonesia Education University. My primary focus lies in game development, where I strive to create engaging experiences that captivate and inspire players.

In addition to game development, I have a diverse skill set that includes photography, videography, and various design techniques. While some of my skills are still at a basic level, others have reached a more advanced stage, allowing me to express my creativity in multiple ways.

I am passionate about continuously learning and growing in the ever-evolving field of multimedia. I invite you to explore my work and join me on this exciting journey!
